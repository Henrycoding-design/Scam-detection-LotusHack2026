Ceramic Pour-Over Set Review

This week we spent time with a handmade ceramic pour-over set from a small studio in Da Lat. The piece feels balanced in the hand, pours cleanly, and encourages a slower morning routine without turning preparation into a chore.

The dripper has a stable base, the glaze softens with daylight, and the matching server keeps heat well for one or two cups. It is the kind of object that quietly improves a kitchen shelf rather than asking for attention.

What We Liked

The ridges inside the brewer kept flow consistent, even with a finer grind. Cleanup was easy, and the handle on the server felt comfortable when pouring multiple cups over breakfast.

The included filters fit neatly, and the soft matte finish paired well with wood and linen accessories on the counter.
