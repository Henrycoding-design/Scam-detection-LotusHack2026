Scam Pattern Demo (Easy → High Risk)

Use this page to inspect patterns from mild suspicious to high-risk scam behavior.

Low Risk (Just Caution)

  • Generic link with unusual domain: http://example-scam-tester.com
  • Cold email style content: "act now" + tight deadline language.
  • Unverified social media share text e.g. "You won't believe this find!"

Medium Risk (Suspicious)

  • Link with mismatched display text: https://secure-login-bank.com
  • Keyword signs: "confirm account", "urgent action required", "vouchers".
  • Fake support contact text: "Reply with your password" (never share credentials).
  • Obfuscated or shortened URL: bit.ly/2XxF2G

High Risk (Strong Scam Indicators)

  • Pretends to be from a bank/government and asks for immediate payment
  • Attachment/fake invoice prompt: "download your refund form"
  • Threat language: "Your account will be closed in 1 hour"
  • Phishing link with brand spoofing: apple-secure-login.com

Critical Risk (Confirmed Scam Pattern)

  • Payment request to cryptocurrency wallet with no invoice control
  • Impersonating trusted contact + urgent request to send money
  • One-time code asks via chat/email for verification (social engineering)
  • Fake tax or refund notice with immediate "confirm identity" form
